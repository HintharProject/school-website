About Us

Empowering Students for a Brighter Tomorrow

Hinthar Education is dedicated to empowering students with high-quality learning experiences that inspire confidence and success.

Located in the heart of Yangon, Myanmar, our institution offers a wide range of educational programs designed to meet the diverse needs of learners at every stage. From Primary and Secondary education to internationally recognized qualifications such as O Level, A Level, and BCS preparation, we provide comprehensive courses delivered by experienced educators committed to excellence.

We believe learning should be engaging, flexible, and accessible. That’s why we offer both on-campus and online classes, enabling students to study in the way that suits them best.

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

expand_more
We offer a variety of courses including Primary, Secondary, O Level, IAL, and BCS preparation.

How can I enroll in a course?

expand_more
You can enroll by visiting our office, contacting us via phone or email, or completing the online registration form on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept bank transfers, cash payments at our office, and mobile payments through popular platforms.

Do you offer online and on-campus classes?

expand_more
Yes, we offer both online and on-campus classes to provide flexible learning options for our students. You can choose the mode that best fits your needs.
